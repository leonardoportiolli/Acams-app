CAMS Cetificationficates

Our certificate courses are a convenient online training for compliance professionals across a range of experience levels, and an option to earn credits towards CAMS certification and re-certification (4 CAMS CREDITS).

In four weeks participants complete a mix of self-paced and live assignments, and receive a Certificate of Achievement.

1- A 30-minute video on the essentials of the certificate subject.

2- Two 90-minute interactive live virtual classrooms.

3- A 15-minute homework.

4- A 15-minute final assessment.

For a full list of certificate courses, please visit:
www.acams.org/certificates

Acams Webinars

Our webinars provide a cost-effective method of delivering mandated AML training without the hassle and added expense of leaving the office.

For those pursuing the CAMS credential, ACAMS webinars are also an option to obtain CAMS credits towards the certification and re-certification.

1 HOUR= 1 ACAMS CREDIT

2 HOURS= 2 ACAMS CREDITS

For upcoming webinars, please visit: www.acams.org/webinars
